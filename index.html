LEVEL 3

BUILDING AI
STEP BY STEP

The 5 main stages of an AI project.

STEP 1

Data Collection

Building the foundation of your project.

  • Collecting historical data (e.g. 100 students).
  • Sources: CSV files, APIs, Surveys.
STEP 2

Data Cleaning

"Garbage In, Garbage Out!"

  • Preparing data for the AI.
  • Removing duplicates & fixing typos.
  • Handling missing data.
STEP 3

Model Training

Teaching the AI to understand trends.

  • Showing cleaned data to algorithms (e.g. Linear Regression).
  • AI develops a mathematical formula for patterns.
STEP 4

Making Predictions

Testing the machine's intelligence.

  • Give model a new value it hasn't seen.
  • AI uses training to calculate the most likely outcome.
STEP 5

Results & Output

Communicating the findings.

Text Result

"Predicted marks: 85%"

Visual Result

Scatter plots & trend lines.

MAIN PROJECT:
PARKINSON'S

A deep dive into healthcare AI.

CONTEXT

Problem Statement

  • Parkinson's affects movement globally.
  • Early symptom: Tremors & handwriting changes.
  • Early detection is difficult but crucial.
CONCEPT

Digital Diagnostics

Using AI to analyze how a person draws or writes.

Non-Invasive: A simple spiral test on a tablet.

TECH

How it Works

🌀
  • Input: Patient draws a spiral image.
  • Pattern: AI looks for pressure & tremor variations.
  • Result: High-accuracy probability score.
TOOLS

The Build Stack

Python (Logic) + OpenCV (Image Processing) + ML (Classification)
